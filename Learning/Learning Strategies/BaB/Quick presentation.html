Welcome to Boost'English

  • Boost'English is an e-learning platform that is designed to be used either as in-class or self-study material.
  • The material is organised into four main themes. The menus can be found at the top of each page - Everyday Communication, Specialist Communication, Professional Communication and Learning Focus.
  • Each theme is organised into a number of learning paths known as ‚Äúbricks‚Äù, which can be completed in any order, according to your needs, your interests or the requirements of your class teacher.

Where to find things?

  • Choose a family (Everyday / Professional / Specialist / Learning Focus).
  • Open a brick (a small module).
  • Inside a brick: Introduction, I start, I learn ... I want more

Types of activities

  • H5P: interactive quizzes, drag & drop, video with questions.
  • LearningApps: matching, ordering, gap-fill.
  • Canva: posters or simple slides.
  • Padlet / Drive: share and comment.

How to work

  • In class:
    • Follow the teacher's instructions
    • Work individually, in pairs or small groups
  • At home:
    • Complete the bricks assigned by your teacher
    • Feel free to explore other bricks that interest you
  • Your ideas matter!
    • You are welcome to suggest new brick topics or activities

Your turn

  • Explore 2-3 bricks now.
  • Pick one thing you liked / unliked and one question.
