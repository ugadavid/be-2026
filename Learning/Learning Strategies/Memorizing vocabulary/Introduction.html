Introduction

CONTENT

You will:

  1. Reflect on your current learning methods, with the help of a short quiz,
  2. Watch a short video,
  3. Test the “word spider web” method of memorising vocabulary,
  4. Read an article and complete a listening comprehension quiz,
  5. You can explore the subject further using the resources provided if you wish.

TIME: approx. 20 mins
CEFR level: B1

At the end of this brick you will be able to apply the techniques illustrated in this brick to help you to learn and retain new vocabulary.
