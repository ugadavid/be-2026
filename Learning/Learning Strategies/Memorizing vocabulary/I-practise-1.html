I practise - Create your own with 'lie' 

Try clustering words around the word lie.

  1. Write any phrase you know with the word lie.
  2. Write any phrase that comes to mind when you say/hear 'lie'.
  3. When you’re stuck look up the word "lie" in an English dictionary in English (preferably)  for learners and add more phrases you didn’t know with the word ‘lie’ in it.

You have just created your first spider web of vocabulary.
Highlight the new meanings of the word for you and try to find relevant contexts where you could use the word, with your best friend, confidant, partner, parent, child, etc (someone that matters to you).
ie: 'you lied to me, you liard! My diary was lying in your drawer all this time!' imagine a scene around those expressions.  If it’s humorous your mind will remember it even better! Imagine all you could say with this word and all its different meanings in this situation or other emotional situations.
