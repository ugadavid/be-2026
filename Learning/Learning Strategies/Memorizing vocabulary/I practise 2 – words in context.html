I practise 2 - words in context

Select a word or expression from the article or explanations of the previous section that is unfamiliar to you. Check its meaning in a dictionary, then use the method from “I practise 1” to build your own spider web of words around the word.

  • check its meaning or meanings
  • connect it to words and phrases you already know
  • Imagine a scene from your everyday experience in which you could use the word. Try to associate people, place, sights and smells with the scene in your imagination.

Suggested words from the text:
critical - immersion - cue - substitute - cluster

Write a text short text describing the scene, including any dialogue.
