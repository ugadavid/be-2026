Introduction

At the end of this brick you will be able to...

  • Identify adjectives in a sentence
  • Tell the difference between adjectives and adverbs
  • Recognize and use common adjectives

CONTENT

You will:

  1. Listen to a song and complete a short exercise.
  2. Watch a video to learn more about adjectives.
  3. Practice key grammar using various types of exercises.
  4. Test your knowledge using a timed quiz
  5. You can explore the subject further using the resources provided if you wish.

TIME: approx. 30 mins
CEFR level: A2
