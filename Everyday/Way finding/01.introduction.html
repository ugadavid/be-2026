Introduction

CONTENT

You will:

  1. watch and listen to see key vocabulary in context
  2. practice this vocabulary using short quizzes
  3. practice the rhythm and intonation of polite question forms
  4. test your knowledge using a timed quiz

TIME: approx. 25 mins
CEFR level: A2-B1

At the end of this brick you will be able to ask for and give directions to a nearby location.
