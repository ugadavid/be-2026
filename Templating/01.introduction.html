Introduction

CONTENT

You will:

  1. Complete a short introductory activity on the topic,
  2. Watch a short video,
  3. Practice key grammar using various types of exercises,
  4. Test your knowledge using a timed quiz,
  5. You can explore the subject further using the resources provided if you wish.

TIME: approx. 30 mins
CEFR level: A2-B1

At the end of this brick you will be able to:

  • Identify and use comparative and superlative adjective forms correctly,
  • Compare two or more people, places, or things using appropriate grammar,
  • Recognize and use irregular adjective forms (e.g., better, worse, the best).
